PROJECT REPORT: RETAIL SALES PERFORMANCE & STRATEGY INSIGHTS
====================================================================

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
------------------
This academic project analyzes retail transaction data to discover 
key sales trends, regional performance, and top product categories 
for Q3 business strategy.

2. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
----------------------------------
* Total Sales: 583K
* Total Transactions: 200
* Unique Customers: 200
* Average Order Value: 2.92K

3. BUSINESS INSIGHTS & ACTION PLAN
----------------------------------
* Online Growth: Online sales are much higher than offline channels. 
  We should focus more on digital marketing to boost revenue.

* Top Regions: A few regions are driving most of our sales. 
  We need to focus on low-performing areas with special discounts.

* Best Products: Our top 5 products bring in the maximum revenue. 
  We must always keep these items in stock to avoid missing sales.

4. TECH STACK USED
------------------
* Database Management: MySQL (Structured Queries & Aggregations)
* Business Intelligence: Power BI (Interactive Dashboard & Data Modeling)
